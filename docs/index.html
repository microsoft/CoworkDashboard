Paste your team's Teams channel link

Where do I find this link in Teams?
  1. Open Microsoft Teams (desktop or web) and go to the channel you created for Cowork reports (e.g. Cowork report - {team}).
  2. Hover the channel name in the left list and click the ⋯ More options button that appears to its right (or right-click the channel).
  3. Choose Get link to channel (older Teams calls it Copy link).
  4. In the dialog, click Copy, then paste it into the box above.

The link looks like https://teams.microsoft.com/l/channel/19%3A…%40thread.tacv2/…?groupId=…. A plain chat link or a browser address-bar URL won't contain the groupId — use Get link to channel so it does.

“Verify” checks the link right here in your browser — it pulls out the team_id, channel_id and channel name, confirms the channel id is a real …@thread.… id and the team id is a valid GUID, and shows them below for you to confirm. It does not contact Teams or sign you in; nothing is sent anywhere. The Download button stays disabled until the link passes these checks.
